THE WHO, WHAT, WHY

What is it?

DroidBeard is a free, open source Android app for managing your SIck Beard installation, either at home or remotely.

Who made it?

DroidBeard was made and is currently maintained by rastating who can be contacted via Twitter or E-mail [PGP Key].
